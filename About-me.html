Tolulope Oke

  • I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at University of Houston-Downtown. I was previously a Teacher-Scholar Postdoctoral fellow and a Visiting Assistant Professor at Wake Forest where I worked with Ellen Kirkman and Frank Moore. 
  • I earned my PhD in Mathematics at Texas A&M University under the guidance of     Sarah Witherspoon . 
  • I was a University Scholar at the University of Ibadan, Nigeria while studying for my Masters degree under the supervision of Michael Enioluwafe.
  • I graduated with a First class honors classification (Summa cum laude) at the University of Ibadan, Nigeria where I completed my Bachelors degree.
  • I am always eager to learn new mathematics with you! Please visit my Service & Mentoring page and let me know what you would like us to read together.
